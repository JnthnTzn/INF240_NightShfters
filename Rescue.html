Contacts of Rescuers

Philippine Animal Welfare Society (PAWS)

  • Focus: A volunteer-based NGO dedicated to preventing animal cruelty through sheltering, advocacy, and education.
  • Contact: (02) 7577-7178 | philpaws@paws.org.ph.
  • Location: Aurora Blvd. cor. Katipunan Valley, Loyola Heights, Quezon City.

PAWSSION Project

  • Focus: A non-profit organization primarily focused on rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming dogs from pounds and distressed situations.
  • Contact: Information is frequently updated via their official Instagram and Facebook pages
  • Location: Bases in Bacolod City and San Jose del Monte, Bulacan

Animal Kingdom Foundation (AKF)

  • Focus: Dedicated to rescuing animals from the illegal dog meat trade and rehabilitating abused animals.
  • Contact: 0939-914-2403 | 0939-914-2405 | hello@akfrescues.org
  • Location: No. 8 Purante St., Brgy. CubCub, Capas, Tarlac (Rescue Center)

Philippine Animal Rescue Team (PART)

  • Focus: A "no-kill" animal rescue organization that manages a large sanctuary for a wide variety of rescued animals.
  • Contact: donorbox.org/sharehope-2 (for support and inquiries)
  • Location: Sanctuary located in Angat, Bulacan

CARA Welfare Philippines

  • Focus: Promotes animal welfare through education, affordable sterilization (Kapon), and the "Trap-Neuter-Vaccinate-Return" (TNVR) program.
  • Contact: 0919-579-0047 | 0905-253-0129 | 8532-3340
  • Location: 175 Lopez Rizal St. cor. Samat St., Mandaluyong City

Help Mandaluyong Animal Shelter (Help MAS Dogs)

  • Focus: A community-led group that provides support for dogs impounded at the Mandaluyong City dog pound.
  • Contact: helpmasdogs@gmail.com
  • Location: Mandaluyong City
